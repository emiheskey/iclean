Frequently Asked Question.

Domestic workers, maids, or housekeepers, we prefer the term "iCleanStars". Either way, they're looking forward to helping you clean your home. Have any questions? To assist, we have outlined some FAQ's, and also included how best to use the platform in different cases.

Q What are your operating times?

We operate Monday to Sunday 7:00am to 6:00pm. However, bookings can be made 24/7 from your mobile, tablet or computer.

Q What is your hourly rate?

Our rate is N1500 per hour. This ensures that cleaners are paid decent wages for the excellent work they do. We run periodic specials and give out vouchers, so keep an eye out for our discounted rates.

Q How do the calculated estimates work?

While the iClean booking platform calculates the estimated amount of time needed to clean your place, please note that you are able to manually change the number of cleaning hours to suit your timeframes and pocket. Note that payments are based on the actual hours it took to do the cleaning and cleaning time should only be adjusted with your consent.

Q How do I book with the same details for a different date and time?

You can quickly and easily make a new booking, based on a completed/previous booking, by selecting the relevant booking under Account -> Bookings. Tap the "Choose Date" button to review the details and select the new date and time.

Q How do the payment works?

Payment information is taken securely online as part of the booking process. Your card details will be used to reserve your appointment. Payment will not be processed until the cleaning has been completed. Payments are currently made by credit or cheque card and this site is protected by a 256-bit SSL encryption, ensuring your data is safe at all times.

Q Can I tip my Cleaner

Yes. After your booking, you will get a chance to rate and tip your cleaner online. Please note, tipping is optional and is enabled when you give a positive rating

Q Can I request the same cleaner for my next booking?

Yes. Step 3 (Date & Time) in the booking process enables you to view and request a cleaner you've previously booked. You can request a cleaner using the "Previous iClean Pro option, and will see her schedule including her available days and times. Note that if you select one of the repeat booking options, you will have the same cleaner come regularly.

Q Is my privacy protected?

We respect your privacy and we will never sell or share your private information. Your credit card details are never stored on our website. This site is protected by a 256-bit SSL encryption, ensuring your data is safe at all times.

Q What do I do if any of my booking information is incorrect?

Please pay careful attention to the details on the booking review page and in the confirmation email. Some of the information can be updated directly via Account -> Bookings. Incorrect bookings can also be cancelled via Account -> Bookings, and a new booking created accordingly.

Q How do I know which cleaner is coming to clean my place?

In the confirmation email sent after you have booked the appointment, we include the name, picture and short bio of the professional cleaner that has been matched to your appointment.

Q I'm not quite comfortable having a stranger at my place. Is my place safe?

Our cleaners go through a rigorous screening process which includes numerous interviews, background and criminal checks, document verification and test cleans. All our cleaners have no less than 1 year of proven cleaning experience. All iClean cleans are fully insured. We give a 100% happiness guarantee on all our cleans, and if you are not 100% satisfied, we will send out someone else at no additional cost

Q How do I book a regular clean?

If you would like to use one of our cleaning professionals once a week, once every two weeks, once every three weeks, or once a month, our repeat cleaning option is for you. To select repeat cleaning, on Step 3 (Date & Time) of the booking process, select how often you would like your place to be cleaned.

Q Need more frequent cleaning

For getting your place cleaned three or more times per week, we also offer reduced rates. For more info, you can email us or call us on 08133646424 and we'll help you get the cleaning appointments setup in the process.

Q How does the cleaner get to my place?

iClean will co-ordinate getting the cleaner to your place. In rare case, there may be transport delays that are out of their control.

Q Do I need to provide lunch for cleaners?

In most cases, your iClean cleaner will bring their own small packed lunch. For longer cleaning appointments, please allow your cleaner to have a brief lunch break.

Q How are cleaning hours divided if I get 2 cleaners?

If 2 cleaners are assigned to your booking, cleaning hours will be split evenly between them. For example, for 10 hours of cleaning, 2 cleaners will do 5 hours each.

Q How do I change my profile information?

To change your personal information, go to Account -> Profile.

Q How do I view, update or cancel my bookings?

To view, cancel or update some of the booking information, go to Account -> Bookings.

Q Is there a late cancellation fee?

Cancelling a booking after 19h00 the day before the appointment will incur a late cancellation fee of N2500 per cleaner assigned to the booking. N500 service fee may still apply. The cancellation fee consists of a cleaner remuneration fee of N1500 for any transport and/or lost earnings, as well as a N1000 administrative fee.

Q How do I update my payment details?

To update your payment details, go to Account -> Profile -> Payment Methods.

Q How do I change my current password?

To change your current password, go to Account -> Profile -> Change Password.

Q Can I purchase a Prepaid or Gift Voucher?

Yes, you can purchase a prepaid / gift voucher with credit or debit card or bank Transfers. You can redeem it yourself or have it sent to whoever you like.

Q Are there rewards for inviting friends?

Yes, we offer rewards in return for each referral. To get started, go to Account -> Refer & Earn.

Q My place is a Total Mess. What should I do?

An obvious case to use iClean is where you have no regular domestic cleaner (or haven't had one for a while) and things are starting to get real in your living space. There are new species of fungi growing on your dirty dishes, and your bedding is used to hide the sheets in addition to covering you up. In this case, enter the particulars of your place as well as additional tasks that need to be done, and schedule a once off clean for a time that suits you. Seeing as you let things get that bad, maybe consider a weekly or once-every-two-weeks clean?

Q I need that Extra shine. Which option should I pick?

While many Nigerians have some form of assistance with domestic cleaning, there are those who are valiant enough to do it all themselves. In this case, you may want a professional cleaner to give the place a deeper clean, doing things like dusting and vacuuming as well as the extras like wiping down walls, oven cleans, fridge cleans and window cleans. In this case, select the amount of rooms you would like cleaned as well as all of the extra tasks that you may not have paid attention to and want done. Observe the hours needed and adjust if necessary. This option can also be used for cases where only a spring clean is needed.

Q I'm moving in or out of my place. What options do I have?

If you're moving in or out of your place, it's likely that there's some additional cleaning to be done. In this case, select the number of rooms to be cleaned as well as additional tasks to be done. If there is a lot of cleaning needed, you may want to use two cleaners. The iClean platform may select two cleaners for bookings longer than 9 hours.

Q I'm having people over. Can you help clean up the mess?

If you are having an event and need a cleaner present either during the event or to clean up afterwards, iClean is here to help. While booking, click on the during/after party button and select the amount of hours required for the clean. Input the address and payment information and then after the process is complete, use "other" under "additional information" to open up a dialogue box telling us that you are specifically booking a cleaner for an event. Alternatively, email us directly at hello@iclean.com.ng and we will set up the booking for you.

Q Do you bring cleaning equipment?

No we don't . Please note that iClean supplies cleaning products (e.g. dish washing liquid) if you have selected this option during the booking process, but not cleaning equipment (e.g. mops, brooms, vacuum cleaners).

Q Is there more information on your cleaning products?

When you select this during the booking process, iClean cleaners will bring a set of general cleaning products to a cleaning appointment. This includes a general all-purpose cleaner, a window cleaner (for windows and mirrors), a bathroom cleaner and dishwashing liquid.

Q How do I stay up to date with the latest news?

Subscribe to our newsletter to get the latest news.
